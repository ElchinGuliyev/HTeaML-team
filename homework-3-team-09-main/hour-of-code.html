Our Hour of Code Experience

"HTeaML" team successfully completed the project “The Hour of Code” at №287 “Zekalar” Lyceum on November 5th, 2024. We started our lesson at 08:00 a.m. with 21 students, and our team did their best. Elchin Guliyev explained 20 steps of 1 lesson in code.org and clarified every movement. Nazrin Jalilova gave brief information about ADA University, and she was taking notes about everything that happened during the lesson. Subhan Jafarli recorded a detailed video about “The Hour of Code”. Ulvi Ramazanli took photos of every moment of our lesson. Besides the duties of each member, all of us helped children with their problems and solved every issue that occurred in their tasks. Overall, the dedication and collaboration of the "HTeaML" team ensured a successful lesson for "The Hour of Code," giving the students at №287 "Zekalar" Lyceum an entertaining and informative experience.
